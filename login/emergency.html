    Emergency Preparedness
    Instruction

    • Earthquake: Drop, Cover, and Hold On. Stay indoors until the shaking stops.
    • Flood: Move to a higher ground immediately. Do not walk or drive through flood waters
    • Hurricane: Evacuate if instructed. Stay away from windows and take shelter in an interior room
    • Wildfire: Evacuate immediately if instructed. Wear protective clothing and cover your face
